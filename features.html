  • Vacuum tube technology
  • Unreliable
  • Supported machine language only
  • very costly
  • Generated a lot of heat and electricity
  • Non Portable
  • Need of AC
  • Huge Size

Some computers of this generation were −

  • ENIAC
  • EDVAC
  • UNIVAC
  • IBM-701
  • IBM-701
